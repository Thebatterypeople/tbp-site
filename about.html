The people behind the power.

The Battery People started the way most good trade businesses do — with someone who saw the job being done slowly and expensively, and knew it could be done better.

Founder Jamie Flynn spent years on the dealership floor in new-car sales, watching customers and workshops alike wait too long and pay too much for something as simple as a battery. So he built the alternative: a mobile-first battery business with no shopfront overheads baked into the price, covering everywhere within 50km of Hallam.

Today The Battery People supply dealerships, workshops and everyday drivers across Melbourne's south-east — with a Hallam retail store on the way.
