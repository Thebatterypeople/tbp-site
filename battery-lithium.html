When lithium is worth it

LiFePO4 lithium runs at a third of the weight, delivers usable capacity right down to near-empty, charges faster, and lasts thousands of cycles. Upfront cost is higher, but per year of service it usually works out cheaper than lead-acid for serious touring, caravan and off-grid setups.
