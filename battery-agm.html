Why AGM matters for stop-start

If your car switches the engine off at the lights, it restarts dozens of times per trip — a standard battery fails fast under that load. AGM (absorbent glass mat) batteries are built for it, with deeper cycling ability and faster charge acceptance. Fitting a standard battery to a stop-start car will void most warranties and can cost you a battery within a year.
